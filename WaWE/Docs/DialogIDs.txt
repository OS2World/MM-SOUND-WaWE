
Used/Reserved Dialog IDs in WaWE:

1 :
The main window of WaWE itself.

3000..3999:
Reserved for usage in WaWE windows, like in main window,
extended file open dialog, etc...

5000..5999:
Dialog IDs of Channel-Set windows and their children

6000..6999:
Entries of Popup menu of WaWE

8000..8999:
Entries in About menu of WaWE

9000..9999:
Entries in Configure menu of WaWE
